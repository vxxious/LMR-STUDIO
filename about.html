About LMRSTUDIO

Our Story

LMRSTUDIO was founded with a passion for artistry and beauty. We believe that makeup is more than just cosmetics, it's an expression of individuality and confidence. Our mission is to help every client feel beautiful, empowered, and ready to conquer the world.

Based in Cotonou, Benin Republic, we've built a reputation for excellence, creativity, and dedication to our craft. From soft glam everyday looks to dramatic evening transformations, our team specializes in a diverse range of makeup styles tailored to each client's unique preferences and skin tone.

Our Philosophy

At LMRSTUDIO, we believe that beauty is personal. We don't believe in one-size-fits-all makeup. Instead, we take time to understand your preferences, your skin type, and your vision. Whether you're preparing for a special event, a photoshoot, or simply want to treat yourself to a professional makeover, we're here to make it happen.

We use only premium, high-quality makeup products and stay up-to-date with the latest techniques and trends in the beauty industry. Our team is trained to work with all skin tones and makeup styles, ensuring everyone feels included and beautiful.

Why Choose LMRSTUDIO?

  • Expert makeup artists with years of experience
  • Personalized consultations for each client
  • Premium products and professional-grade tools
  • Wide range of makeup styles from soft glam to dramatic
  • Studio and home service options available
  • Affordable pricing without compromising quality

Meet the Founder
