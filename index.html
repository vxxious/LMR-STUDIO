Booking Policy

Please read our terms and conditions

Booking Information

All appointments must be booked in advance. We recommend booking at least 48 hours before your desired date to ensure availability.

Deposits

A non-refundable deposit of 50% is required to secure your booking. The remaining balance is due on the day of service. Deposits can be paid via mobile money or bank transfer.

Timing & Punctuality

Please arrive 10 minutes before your scheduled appointment. Late arrivals may result in shortened service time or rescheduling. If you're running late, please contact us immediately.

Guest Policy

For studio appointments, we ask that you limit guests to maintain a professional and focused environment. For home service appointments, please ensure a clean, well-lit area is available for the makeup application.

Date Changes & Cancellations

If you need to reschedule, please provide at least 24 hours notice. Cancellations made less than 24 hours before your appointment will forfeit the deposit. Rescheduling is subject to availability.

Home Service Terms

Home service appointments require a valid address within the Cotonou area. Additional travel fees may apply for locations outside the standard service area. Please ensure adequate lighting and a suitable workspace is available.

Student Discount

Student pricing is available with valid student ID presented at the time of booking and appointment. This discount cannot be combined with other offers.

Health & Safety

Please inform us of any allergies, skin sensitivities, or medical conditions that may affect the makeup application. We use professional, high-quality products, but cannot guarantee against all allergic reactions.
