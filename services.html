Our Services

Explore our range of professional makeup styles tailored to your unique style

Soft Glam

A natural, elegant look perfect for everyday wear, professional settings, and anyone who prefers a subtle enhancement. Features neutral tones, well-blended eyeshadow, and a radiant complexion that enhances your natural beauty without being too bold.

Bratz

Bold, doll-like features inspired by the iconic Bratz dolls. This style features dramatic, oversized eyes with intense eyeshadow, bold winged eyeliner, and glossy lips. Perfect for making a statement and turning heads at any event.

Barbie

Glamorous, pink-toned makeup with a flawless finish inspired by the Barbie aesthetic. Features soft pink eyeshadows, rosy cheeks, glossy pink lips, and a radiant glow. Ideal for those who want a feminine, playful, and polished look.

Dark (Black Eyeshadow)

Sultry smokey eyes with dramatic black eyeshadow perfect for evening events and special occasions. This bold look features deep, rich blacks with blended transitions, creating a mysterious and sophisticated appearance.

Baddie Beat

Bold, confident look with sharp contours and striking features. This power makeup style features strong definition, sharp eyeliner wings, bold eyebrows, and an overall confident vibe. Perfect for anyone who wants to feel empowered and fierce.

Paint Me Down

Full glam with artistic flair and intricate detailing. This service allows for creative expression with detailed art, special effects, and artistic techniques. Perfect for creative individuals, themed events, and those who want something truly unique.

Extra Blush

Rosy, sun-kissed glow with emphasis on fresh, youthful radiance. Features prominent blush placement for a youthful, healthy appearance with emphasis on the cheeks. Perfect for those who want a bright, approachable, and radiant look.

Custom Design

Tell us your vision and we'll create a custom makeup look tailored specifically to you. Whether you're combining styles or creating something entirely new, our team works with you to bring your makeup dreams to life.
