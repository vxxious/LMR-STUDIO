Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in each package?

Each package includes professional makeup application using premium products, professional consultation to understand your preferences, and guidance on maintaining your makeup throughout the day.

Can I customize my booking?

Absolutely! We offer custom packages for group bookings, special events, and unique requests. Please contact us via WhatsApp for personalized quotes.

How long does a session take?

Sessions typically range from 1 to 2 hours depending on the style complexity and your preferences. More intricate designs may require additional time.

Do you offer group rates?

Yes! Special rates are available for bridal parties, group bookings, and corporate events. Contact us for a custom quote.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept mobile money, bank transfers, and cash. A 50% deposit is required to secure your booking.
