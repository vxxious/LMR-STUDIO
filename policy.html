Booking Policy & Terms

Please read our terms and conditions carefully

Booking Information

All appointments must be booked in advance through our website, WhatsApp, or by calling us directly. We recommend booking at least 48 hours before your desired appointment date to ensure availability. For rush bookings or same-day appointments, please contact us via WhatsApp for immediate assistance.

Deposits & Payment

A non-refundable deposit of 50% is required to secure your booking. The remaining balance is due on the day of service in full before or immediately after your appointment.

Payment Methods Accepted:

  • Mobile money transfers (MTN, Moov, etc.)
  • Bank transfers
  • Cash (at time of appointment)

Timing & Punctuality

Please arrive 10 minutes before your scheduled appointment time. This allows us to discuss your preferences and prepare for your service.

Late Arrivals: If you arrive more than 15 minutes late, we may need to reschedule your appointment to accommodate other clients. Late arrivals may result in shortened service time at no reduced cost.

Contact Policy: If you're running late, please contact us immediately via WhatsApp or phone.

Guest Policy

Studio Appointments: For studio-based appointments, we ask that you limit guests to one companion to maintain a professional environment and ensure we can focus on your beauty service.

Home Service Appointments: For home service appointments, please ensure:

  • A clean, well-lit, comfortable space is available
  • Adequate table or vanity space for makeup application
  • A good mirror and natural or bright artificial lighting
  • Minimal distractions during the appointment

Date Changes & Cancellations

Rescheduling: If you need to change your appointment date or time, please provide at least 24 hours notice. Rescheduling is subject to availability and will be confirmed by our team.

Cancellation Policy:

  • Cancellations made 24+ hours before appointment: Full deposit refund
  • Cancellations made less than 24 hours before appointment: Deposit forfeited
  • No-shows: Deposit forfeited and full amount due upon rescheduling

In case of emergency, please contact us as soon as possible.

Home Service Terms

Service Area: Home service appointments are available within the Cotonou area and surrounding neighborhoods.

Travel Fees: Standard home service pricing includes travel within the main Cotonou area. Additional travel fees may apply for locations outside the standard service area. These will be communicated when booking.

Workspace Requirements: Please ensure adequate lighting (natural or bright artificial), a table or vanity with a mirror, and a comfortable chair for the appointment duration.

Student Discount

Student pricing is available at 6,000 CFA with valid student identification presented at the time of booking confirmation and on the day of appointment.

Requirements:

  • Valid student ID from recognized institution
  • Current enrollment status
  • Cannot be combined with other promotional offers

Health & Safety

Your health and safety are our top priorities. Please inform us of any:

  • Allergies to cosmetic products
  • Skin sensitivities or conditions
  • Recent skin treatments or procedures
  • Medical conditions that may affect makeup application
  • Medications that may cause skin sensitivity

Important Note: We use professional-grade, high-quality makeup products suitable for most skin types. However, we cannot guarantee against all possible allergic reactions. By booking with us, you acknowledge that you have disclosed all relevant health information.

Hygiene Standards: We maintain strict hygiene standards and use sanitized applicators and tools for every client. All products are genuine and obtained from authorized suppliers.

Makeup Guarantee

We take pride in our work and guarantee professional-quality makeup application. If you're not satisfied with your makeup on the day of service, please inform us immediately so we can make adjustments.

Touch-ups & Corrections: Minor adjustments and corrections can be made during your appointment. Touch-ups made after leaving the studio may be charged as a new service.

Liability Disclaimer

LMRSTUDIO is not responsible for:

  • Damage to clothing or accessories during appointments
  • Allergic reactions that may occur after appointment completion
  • Makeup appearance changes due to client activities post-appointment
  • Client dissatisfaction due to unrealistic expectations

By booking with LMRSTUDIO, you accept these terms and acknowledge our disclaimer of liability.

Special Requests & Custom Designs

We welcome special requests and custom makeup designs. Please communicate your vision clearly during consultation. Complex designs may require additional time and potentially additional fees, which will be discussed before booking confirmation.

For special occasion makeup (bridal, events, photoshoots), we recommend booking earlier and scheduling a consultation beforehand.

Policy Updates

LMRSTUDIO reserves the right to update this policy at any time. Clients will be notified of significant changes via WhatsApp or email. Your continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of these terms.
